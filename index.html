About the Workshop

Welcome to the world’s first "Invention Capture & Patenting Hackathon," a unique 3-day event to help participants learn to draft and file patents under expert guidance. Innovators, startups, researchers, and students will be trained to transform ideas into provisional patents, gain knowledge about funding opportunities, and understand the economic significance of their inventions.
